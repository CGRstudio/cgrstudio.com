About

CGR Studio is the practice of Cameron Robertson — a design consultant and visualization specialist based in Boynton Beach, Florida.

With a Bachelor of Architecture from Florida Atlantic University and seven years of experience across residential remodel production, construction documentation, and 3D visualization, Cameron works directly with general contractors and remodeling teams to bridge the gap between a client's vision and what gets built.

The studio's focus is practical: deliver design clarity that helps GCs close clients, reduce miscommunication on site, and deliver better results — faster.
